About Me

I’ve always had an eye for style and colors. My interest in fashion sparked in my early teens and gradually led me down the path of appreciating art in all its forms. That creativity evolved into Tiedyes.ke, a brand I used express myself by bringing simple, vibrant art to life on t-shirts — each one a little piece of my personality.

I am currently purusing a degree in Software Engineering at the African Leadership University, a skill that will be useful in my content journey aside from my mission of incorporating tech in fighting climate change. For my internship at Untapped Global , I'm currently focusing on front-end web development to expand my digital skillset.

My passion for content creation is deeply tied to my love for traveling — I take at least one trip every two months, which helps me build my mindset and appreciate the gifts of life. I enjoy reviewing and critiquing restaurants and destinations, giving my honest take on places to visit.

On my TikTok (@abbie5085), I recently launched a series called "Living" — where I share my point of view while staying in various locations for at least a week. Viewers are encouraged to comment and ask questions about each place or city I feature.

Although I don’t do content creation professionally, I create and share content out of passion. I’ve voluntarily created content for brands like La Roche to share my skincare journey, and for SmartGyms and Fitness Point Gym to document my fitness journey since 2023. I’ve also promoted events for brands such as Tufike Entertainment, known for lighting up Nairobi’s night scene.

I recently began horse riding as a hobby, where I actively participate at Achis Ranch in Karen or occasionally at The Jockey Club of Kenya. I also enjoy attending pop-up markets like the Farmers Market and the Pop Up Market at the Jockey Club — places where I get to engage in two of my favorite things at once: discovering new products and enjoying good experiences. Overall, I’d describe myself as an outgoing person who enjoys anything music and art related.
